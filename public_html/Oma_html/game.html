The Story Begins

First scene

Hero wakes up in a cruise ship and hears a voice trough the speaker: ""Rise and shine! Hero. I want to play a game"

“You're probably wondering where you are. I'll tell you where you might be. You might be in the room that you die in.” “So, are you going to watch yourself die here today, Hero, or do something about it?" “I have a test for you, I found that last night in Cosmopolitan. “

“I will ask you questions and you must pick the right one.” “If you pass the test, I will let you live. “

Hero is confused and decides to go to explore the ship. It seems that most of the passengers are either dead or turned into zombies.

Game Map

The Game Map was designed and made by Tero. He used Tiled Map Editor for creating the Map.

Tiled is a 2D level editor that helps users to develop the content of their game. Its primary feature is to edit tile maps of various forms, but it also supports free image placement as well as powerful ways to annotate level with extra information used by the game.

The cabin scene

When you enter the cabin scene

Questions Panel

After you enter the cabin , you can explore it in peace and collect items that you may need. When you try to exit the cabin , the voice on the speaker locks the door and asks you to answer a question. You have to answer correctly to unlock the door.

Interaction with objects

Our Hero is able to interact with objects. He can collect items in to the Inventory and use them. Hero can also interact with other characters like the Hot Blond. Hero can also eat the hamburger when the item is added into the Inventory.
