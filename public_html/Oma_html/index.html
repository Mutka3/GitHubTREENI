Game Idea

Game story summary

Our story takes place on a cruise ship in the middle of the Baltic Sea. The passengers just wanted to have an amazing vacation and relax on a luxury cruise ship. The crew were just doing its daily job and trying to make the passengers’ trip amazing. Hero was dancing at a dance club. He found a girl that he decides to take to his cabin to have some fun. Suddenly the girl said that she didn’t feel okay. She slowly struggled and turned into a zombie and tried to kill the hero. Hero reached out to take a vodka bottle from the table to smash it to the girls’ head, killing the suddenly metamorphosed zombie. He was so confused that he stumbled to the body and hit his head in the corner of the table. When he wakes up, power seems to be off and the entire ship is now full of zombies.

The main goal

The captain's bridge is the main goal. There our hero can contact 911 via radio to call an emergency helicopter and/or ship to the rescue. The engine room is the place to get power back on to have the radios working. The hero’s role is to figure out how he got on the ship in the first place and how to escape alive from the cruise ship. For that he needs to figure out how to contact the outside world (if there is one left ) and call for help.

Original gameplay idea

Hero has 2-3 options or items to choose from. One of them is the right one. If you have been watching horror films, you know that in every movie, bad things happen in an order and there are some rules you must follow if hero want's to survive.

Example: The voice in the speaker: So do you want to play a game ?

  1. Scream
  2. Say: “Hello”
  3. Say nothing

If hero chose:

  • (Scream): (You are going to die... next time, save your breath for a blow-up doll or something)
  • If you chose (“Hello”): Don’t walk around saying “Hello” to a murderer because he is not going to respond “Yeah I’m in the kitchen. Want a sandwich?”
  • If you chose (Say nothing): You survived this one // (Oh you like to play hide and seek with the murderer… is it really a good idea? but yeah you survived this one?)

Hero chose to be quiet and say nothing but what’s next? Hero needs something…

  1. Lamp
  2. Umbrella
  3. Gun

If hero chose

  • Lamp (What were you thinking?) Try again!
  • Umbrella (Really? Yeah, it’s going to rain… It’s going to rain your BLOOD !!!) Try again!
  • Gun (No s%#! Sherlock... you got it right)

At the last moment we actually decided to use the questions that we had planned at the start of the project.
